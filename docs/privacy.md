# Chiva Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Chiva is an offline dominoes score tracker. The app helps you record game scores, hand outcomes, and completed game history on your device.

## Data Collection

Chiva does not collect personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not include ads, does not track users, and does not transmit game history to a server.

## Local Storage

Chiva stores active and completed game data locally on your device so your score history is available when you reopen the app. This data stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear the app's data through your device settings.

## Third Parties

Chiva does not share data with third parties.
