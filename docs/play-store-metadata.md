## Listing Copy

Short description:

Dominoes score tracker for fast hand-by-hand scoring.

Full description:

Chiva is a clean score tracker for Puerto Rican and Caribbean dominoes games. Track hands, totals, bonuses, prizes, Chiva wins, and completed game summaries without accounts or setup.

Designed for quick scoring at the table, Chiva keeps game entry simple and shows the current score clearly after every hand.
